About Us

Destinations

A century of glory can be viewed and admired, feasting your eyes.

Start from HGSH

Pryvit! Tymofii is a 19-year-old exchange student from NYCU, majoring in computer science. We started from Hsinchu Girls’ Senior High School and began our journey!

1.Yin Hsi East Gate

The East Gate is the only remaining gate in Hsinchu with over 200 years of history. After some renovation, it became a popular landmark for casual stroll or dance practices.

2.Dongmen Market

Dongmen Market contains various types of stores. Been through a few renovations, in 2015, it was painted by a group of teachers and students and became a popular spot.

3.OR House

Back in the 1920s, it was the first department store in Hsinchu. It reopened at the end of last year and regained popularity as an attraction containing both history and modernity.

4.814大同Ice Pop

It was actually derived from a group of laborers from a tobacco factory named 814 to maintain income after the factory closed. It serves traditional popsicles with ten flavors. Yummy!

5.Chenghuang Temple

Chenghuang judges people's good and bad deeds with two assistants who represent loyalty and brotherhood. Food markets around the temple made it a unique destination in Hsinchu.

6.Beimen Street

On the streets, buildings with styles across hundreds of years can be seen. Thanks to the government's preservation, the glory of the first high street in Hsinchu can be saved.

7.Hsinchu City Government

Constructed in 1927, the city government has been through many reconstruction and expansion, including western arcades and Japanese black tiles.
